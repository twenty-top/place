Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-02

Ping Pong Arcade ("we", "us", "our") operates a free arcade gaming platform. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency in how we handle your data.

What We Collect

  • Contact Information: If you contact us via our contact form, we collect the information you provide, such as your name and email address, solely to respond to your inquiries.
  • Gameplay Data: Our games use your browser's localStorage to save high scores. This data is stored exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Aggregated Analytics: We collect basic, anonymous usage data, such as page views and session counts, to help us improve our website and services. This data is always aggregated and cannot be used to identify you.

How We Use Information

  • To operate and maintain the website and its games.
  • To respond to your support requests and messages.
  • To analyze usage patterns for improving site features, game balance, and bug fixes.

Local Storage & Scores

High scores are saved in your browser's localStorage. You have full control over this data and can clear it at any time from our Stats page or by clearing your browser's site data for Ping Pong Arcade.

Cookies

We use a minimal number of essential cookies for core site functionality and anonymous analytics. For more details, please review our Cookie Policy.

Data Security

We employ reasonable security measures to protect data. However, no online transmission or storage method is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your email account if you choose to contact us.

Children

Our Service is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, please email us at: support@pingpongarcade.com
