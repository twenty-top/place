FAQ

Do I need an account?
No. Just open a game and start playing instantly.

Where are my scores saved?
Scores are stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage. This means your data stays on your device. You can clear your scores anytime from the "All Games" or "Stats" pages.

Is it really free?
Yes, 100% free. We are a passion project dedicated to providing quality educational entertainment without cost.

Can you add [X] game?
We're always open to suggestions! Use our contact form to recommend a classic arcade game you'd love to see here. We focus on games that are lightweight and work well on the web.
